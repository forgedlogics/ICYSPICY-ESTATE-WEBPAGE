At the Heart of Vallabh Vidyanagar’s Educational & Commercial Hub

Positioned within walking distance of Charutar Vidya Mandal (CVM) and the iconic Shastri Maidan, this commercial address enjoys a strategic location in the heart of Vallabh Vidyanagar—India’s first planned university township. Surrounded by renowned educational institutions, professional colleges, research centres, hospitals, and thriving residential neighbourhoods, the area benefits from consistent daily footfall generated by students, faculty, professionals, residents, and visitors.

Its central location offers excellent visibility, strong connectivity, and a business environment that supports retail, dining, healthcare, professional services, education, wellness, and office spaces alike. The proximity to one of Gujarat’s most respected educational ecosystems ensures sustained commercial activity and long-term growth potential, making it an exceptional destination for businesses, investors, and entrepreneurs seeking a prestigious and future-ready address.
